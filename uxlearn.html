A design that shows the basic principles a UX designer must adhere to. The layout is responsive, readable and clean. It features a visually appealing font style and size, that is eaisly adaptive to different screen sizes.
The colors are used such that they highlight the title of the content and yet are not very distractive while reading the content text. They mark separations between 2 different topics.
